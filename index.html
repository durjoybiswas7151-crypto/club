About Us

Chowdhuripara Club is a community-based club formed by young people with a vision to improve our society.

We focus on:

Sports & Cultural Programs

Organizing sports tournaments and cultural programs that bring our community together

Community Service

Helping local people through meaningful social work and community initiatives

Education & Values

Supporting education and promoting moral values throughout our community

Positive Guidance

Encouraging everyone to stay away from bad habits and follow the right path

Our club believes in teamwork, honesty, and development.

Sports & Activities

We arrange various sports and social activities every month to spread positivity, friendship, and community awareness.

Football & Cricket

Regular tournaments that bring together players from across our community, fostering healthy competition and teamwork.

Environmental Care

Cleaning campaigns and tree plantation programs to keep our area beautiful and environmentally sustainable.

Blood Donation

Life-saving blood donation events that help our community members in times of medical emergencies.

Cultural Programs

Traditional and modern cultural events that celebrate our heritage and bring joy to the community.

Youth Leadership

Leadership development programs that empower young people to become positive change-makers in society.

Social Awareness
